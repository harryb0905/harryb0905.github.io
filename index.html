Fish.com

Having a pond in your own garden will require constant monitoring and great care. So I decided to create a wireless livestream using a Raspberry Pi, which would allow any user in our house to view the livestream 24/7 on any device connected to the internet. The aquatic life in the pond can be viewed and reminds the user of when the fish were last fed.
